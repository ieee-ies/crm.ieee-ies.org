TC Control, Robotics, and Mechatronics

Welcome to the IEEE-IES Technical Committee on Control, Robotics, and Mechatronics (TC-CRM) website!

We propose papers and publications, organize and operate special sessions, tracks, tutorials, provide volunteers to review papers within our scope at IEEE IES flagship conferences.

If you are a researcher or a practitioner and your interests include

  • robot control
  • bilateral control of master slave systems
  • internet based control (contol of time-delayed systems)
  • robot technology middleware
  • telemanipulation, telerobotics and telepresence systems
  • intelligent space
  • emerging technologies for mechatronics applications (cognitive and etho-robotics, human system interactioin, SME robots)
then this Technical Committee is for you. Membership is free.

If you wish to be involved in these activities, join the TC-CRM.
